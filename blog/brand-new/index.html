Brand New

Feb 2nd, 2014

Hello. My name is Longsley and I am brand new. I am hoping to build from scratch a network of like-minded individuals who share my passion for the use of technology to improve the world. I believe war and inequality can be made obsolete. I hope to explore these and other topics through blogging and other online social activities. I also aim to do this completely anonymously.

My reasons for maintaining my anonymity are varied and complex and will be explored in future blog posts. The short explanation is that I can truly speak my mind only if I don’t have to worry about how my views and words will affect the ones I love.

To secure my anonymity, I have endeavored to only connect to services related to Longsley via Tor. I have settled for a Yandex email address as the major webmail providers require phone numbers these days. My Twitter account is currently suspended (likely due to connecting through Tor and signing up with a Yandex email address…), but I have filed an appeal and have every expectation that it will be reinstated. I have also kept completely silent about this project to anyone with whom I have a personal connection.

My anonymity is not perfect. The Tor network could be compromised. My hardware or software could be backdoored. I could eventually make a mistake and access Longsley-related services in an insecure manner. The important thing is that I’ve taken the precautions I am willing to take and I am now confident enough to go to work.

I need your help

As Longsley, I have no contacts. I know no one. I have words and ideas and passion and ambition and brains and guts, but I have no influence. I can publish this post and literally no one will see it.
